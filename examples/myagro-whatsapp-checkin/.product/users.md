# Target Users

## Overview

- **Farmer**: Farmer who buys myAgro inputs. He/she has a low education level, not very familiar with technology and does not have regular stable income. 
- **Field agent**: A myAgro agent that goes on the field. He has a mobile app that he uses to register farmers, assign them packages and helps them make payments.

## 1. Farmer

**Description:** Farmer who buys myAgro inputs. He/she has a low education level, not very familiar with technology and does not have regular stable income. 

**Tech Comfort:** Low

**Location/Context:** Leaves in villages in Senegal

### Goals

- He wants to be able to easily enroll for myAgro packages with minimal effort

### Pain Points

- "He/she has no easy way to sign up for myAgro packages. Currently he needs to be enrolled by a field agent"

## 2. Field agent

**Description:** A myAgro agent that goes on the field. He has a mobile app that he uses to register farmers, assign them packages and helps them make payments.

**Tech Comfort:** High

**Location/Context:** He lives in a city but will be working in the same villages as the farmer

### Goals

- Have an easy to enroll farmers 

### Pain Points

- "He has too many farmers to enroll on the same day"
